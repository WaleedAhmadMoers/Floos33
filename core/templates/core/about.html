About floos33

A prototype B2B marketplace for liquidation and overstock lots.

floos33 is a focused prototype to test how sellers can publish liquidation or overstock lots and how buyers can quickly assess them. We emphasize clear listing data, trustworthy seller profiles, and a simple navigation experience.

This demo is static and does not process real transactions. It is designed to validate flows for listing creation, browsing, and contact readiness before building a production system.
